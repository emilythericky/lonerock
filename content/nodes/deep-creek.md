## Depth

**"Attempted to escape by hiding in the mines the last 9 days. Good Time Reinstated."**
Transcribed from *The Tracy City News*, date approximate, 1894-1896. *Good Time* referred to a reduction of sentence for good behavior. Reinstated means it was revoked as punishment for the escape attempt. The sentence was therefore extended. This is the full record.

**Primitive Baptists in Grundy County**
The Primitive Baptist Church maintains a continuous presence in Grundy County. Their practice of creek baptism — total immersion in natural waterways — continues. They are a separate tradition from missionary Baptist churches, rejecting Sunday schools, paid clergy, and organized missions. They practice foot-washing. They believe in predestination.

**Algal bloom indicators**
Algal blooms in the Grundy Lakes ponds indicate elevated nutrient levels, possibly from coal ash leaching combined with agricultural and residential runoff. *Microcystis aeruginosa*, the cyanobacterium responsible for most harmful blooms in Tennessee, produces microcystin, a hepatotoxin. Swimming advisories are issued irregularly.
