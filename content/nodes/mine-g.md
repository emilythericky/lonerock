## Depth

**Mine G, Lone Rock**
One of multiple numbered mines operated by TCIR in the Lone Rock complex. The mines ran horizontal drifts into the coal seams of the Cumberland Plateau escarpment. Conditions: poor ventilation, high methane, inadequate timber supports, no safety enforcement. Mortality records are incomplete.

**Naloxone (Narcan)**
Opioid overdose reversal medication. Available over-the-counter in Tennessee since 2015. Grundy County's overdose death rate exceeds the state average, which exceeds the national average. The county has a single hospital. Nearest Level 1 trauma center: Erlanger in Chattanooga, approximately 50 miles.

**Fiddlehead ferns**
*Matteuccia struthiopteris* and *Osmunda regalis* both colonize disturbed and enriched soils. High nitrogen availability — from organic matter decomposition — accelerates growth. They do not bioaccumulate heavy metals at rates that make them unsafe to eat, unlike some species. This distinction matters for the people who forage here.
